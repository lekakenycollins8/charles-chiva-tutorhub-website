# Chiva TutorHub Website Structure

## Navigation Structure

### Main Navigation
- **Home** - Landing page with key information and video showcase
- **About** - Information about the tutor and tutoring philosophy
- **Services** - Details about tutoring services offered
- **Pricing** - Service pricing plans and payment options
- **Resources** - Educational materials (free and paid)
- **Blog** - Educational articles and updates
- **Contact** - Contact form and information

## Page Content Structure

### Home Page
- **Hero Section**
  - Headline: "Expert Tutoring in Chemistry, Mathematics, Business, and Accounting"
  - Subheadline: "Personalized learning to help you achieve academic success"
  - Call-to-action buttons: "Explore Services" and "Contact Now"
  - Professional image of tutor or tutoring session

- **Services Overview**
  - Brief cards for each service area with icons
  - "Learn More" links to individual service pages

- **Testimonials**
  - Success stories from students
  - Before/after grade improvements

- **Video Showcase**
  - Featured tutoring video or introduction
  - Brief explanation of teaching style

- **Quick Contact**
  - Mini contact form
  - Chat widget popup

### About Page
- **Tutor Introduction**
  - Professional photo
  - Brief personal statement
  - Qualifications and experience

- **Educational Background**
  - Degrees and certifications
  - Specialized training

- **Tutoring Experience**
  - Years of experience
  - Success stories
  - Areas of expertise

- **Tutoring Philosophy**
  - Teaching approach
  - Learning methods
  - Student success focus

- **Why Choose Chiva TutorHub**
  - Key differentiators
  - Benefits of working with this tutor

### Services Pages
Each service (Chemistry, Mathematics, Business, Accounting) will have:

- **Service Overview**
  - Description of the service
  - Who it's for (high school/college)
  - Expected outcomes

- **Topics Covered**
  - Detailed list of subjects within each area
  - Breakdown by subtopics

- **Teaching Approach**
  - Methods used for this specific subject
  - Tools and resources provided

- **Pricing Options**
  - Service-specific pricing if applicable
  - Link to main pricing page

### Pricing Page
- **Pricing Plans**
  - Basic, Standard, and Premium options
  - Clear breakdown of what's included
  - Price per hour/week/month

- **Custom Packages**
  - Information about custom tutoring packages
  - Contact link for custom quotes

- **Payment Options**
  - Accepted payment methods
  - Information about Stripe checkout

### Resources Page
- **Resource Categories**
  - Filtering by subject area
  - Filtering by free/paid

- **Resource Listings**
  - Thumbnail/preview
  - Title and brief description
  - Free/paid indicator
  - Download/purchase button

- **Featured Resources**
  - Highlighted study materials
  - Most popular downloads

### Blog Page
- **Blog Categories**
  - Filter by subject area
  - Filter by topic type (study tips, subject deep dives, etc.)

- **Blog Listings**
  - Featured image
  - Title
  - Publication date
  - Brief excerpt
  - "Read More" link

- **Individual Blog Posts**
  - Full article content
  - Author information
  - Related posts
  - Social sharing options

### Contact Page
- **Contact Form**
  - Name, email, subject fields
  - Message box
  - Subject dropdown (general inquiry, tutoring request, etc.)

- **Contact Information**
  - Email address
  - Phone number
  - Social media links

- **Office Hours**
  - Availability for tutoring sessions
  - Time zone information

## Admin Dashboard Structure

### Admin Login
- Secure login page for administrator

### Dashboard Home
- Overview of site statistics
- Recent messages
- Recent resource uploads
- Recent blog posts

### Resource Management
- Upload new resources
- Edit existing resources
- Delete resources
- Toggle free/paid status

### Blog Management
- Create new blog posts
- Edit existing posts
- Delete posts
- View post analytics

### Message Center
- View incoming messages from chat and contact form
- Reply to messages
- Mark messages as read/unread
- Archive old messages

### Video Management
- Upload videos for homepage
- Select featured video
- Archive old videos
